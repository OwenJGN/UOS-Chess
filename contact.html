FAQs

Common Questions

Do I need to be a student?

No — our club nights are primarily for UOS students and staff, but our tournaments are open to the general public. Contact us to find out more.

What level do I need to be?

Any level is welcome. We have complete beginners through to highly-rated players, and our coaching programme caters to everyone.

How do I join the society?

Sign up through the University of Southampton Students' Union (SUSU) website, then come along to any club night — no prior booking needed.

Is there a membership fee?

A small annual SUSU society membership fee applies. Tournament entry is free for UOS students. Check our Discord for current details.
