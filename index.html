Rules & Regulations

♟ FIDE Laws of Chess

All games are played under current FIDE Laws of Chess.

⏱ Time Control

90 minutes plus 30-second increment from move one.

📵 Electronic Devices

Devices must be switched off and out of sight.

✋ Touch-Move Rule

Touch-move rules apply throughout the event.

📝 Recording Moves

Moves must be recorded in rated sections.

🤝 Conduct & Fair Play

Sportsmanship and respect are expected.

⚖ Draw Offers

Draw offers must follow FIDE procedure.

📣 Appeals & Protests

Appeals must be submitted within 30 minutes.
