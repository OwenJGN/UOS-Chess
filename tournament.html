About the Tournament

Format

  • Swiss system — all players complete every round
  • 5 rounds per section
  • U2050 and U1700: FIDE & ECF rated, 90+30 time control
  • U1300: ECF rated only, 60+30 time control
  • Optional Blitz Tournaments on Thursday and Saturday (extra fee)

  • U1300 — ECF Rated Only
