Overview

About the Tournament

The Spring Open Chess Championship is UOS Chess Society's flagship annual event, open to students, staff, and the wider community. Whether you're picking up a piece for the first time or a seasoned club player, there's a section for you.

Format

  • Swiss system — all players complete every round
  • 5 rounds per section
  • Classical time controls (45+15)
  • FIDE-rated (Open section)
  • Arbiters on duty throughout

Sections

  • Open — 1600+ rated / experienced players
  • Intermediate — Under 1600 / club regulars
  • Beginners — New to tournament play

Prizes

  • 🥇 Open 1st — £150 + trophy
  • 🥈 Open 2nd — £75
  • 🥉 Open 3rd — £40
  • 🏆 Best Junior prize
  • 🏅 Section winners — trophies

Venue & Facilities

University of Southampton — main campus.

Chess sets, boards, and clocks provided.
Refreshments available on site.
Free parking for participants.

What to Bring

Rules & Fair Play

Tournament Rules

General Rules

  • FIDE Laws of Chess apply throughout
  • Mobile phones must be silenced and stored
  • No engine assistance permitted
  • Arbiter decisions are final
  • Players must record moves in all rounds

Bye Policy

  • Half-point byes available on request
  • Byes must be requested before pairings are made
  • Maximum one bye per player
